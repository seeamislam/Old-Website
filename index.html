The Freezys

A novel treatment for any Gout patient looking for a safe, yet effective treatment.

Problem:
Gout patients suffer from excessive pain within their MTP joint (big toe joint). Ultimately, this triggers intense swelling and inflammation.

Design Metrics:
This revolutionary product is purposed to apply ammonium nitrate (ice gel packs) to your swollen joint. The current temperature of the ice is sent to your phone via bluetooth. Then, using the Ardudroid App on the Play Store, the device will constantly send your phone updates regarding the temperature of the ammonium nitrate.
Furthermore, if the user does not own a phone, there is a notification system integrated within the device. If the ice is below its melting point (0 degrees), a green LED will turn on. If it is above melting point , a red LED will turn on.

Therefore, you can monitor the condition of your tool 24/7 using both the LEDs and the bluetooth functionality.

Collatz Conjecture

Collatz Conjecture is a mathematical phenomenon that has left mathematicians curious for dozens of years. The algorithm is as follows: Take any positive integer. If even, divide by 2. If odd, multiple by 3 then add 1. Repeat the process.

Regardless of the initial number, you will always conclude with a sequence of 4, 2, then 1.
For example: When 55 is inputted, it requires 113 procedural repetitions in order to conclude with 1. Mathematicians ponder over the explanation as to why this phenomenon occurs.
